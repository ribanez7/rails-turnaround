About

Rubén hails from Terrassa, but he lives in Barcelona. He has earned degrees from the University of Barcelona, and some other titulations from different accademies. Before starting as a Devops for software architecture, he worked for Onelink as a web developer and web operations. He thinks that Ruby is the most beautiful programming language ever, but never forgets that bash, the unfriendly friend, knows how to do a lot of things efficiently, you just have to count on him. The command line is his natural biosphere.
